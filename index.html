Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How long does it take to build a website?

Most projects are live within 2 weeks of kickoff. Complex e‑commerce platforms may take 3–4 weeks, but I always deliver on the timeline we agree on.

Q. What's included in the price?

Custom design, basic SEO setup, copywriting, and hosting & domain setup , no hidden add-ons. You get a fully functional, production‑ready website.

Q. Are there ongoing costs after the build?

Just hosting and domain renewal, which I'll help you set up affordably. Hosting typically costs between KSh 2,000 – 5,000 per year depending on your provider.

Q. What if I need changes after launch?

Free revisions every 3 months after launch, plus real performance data so you can see what's working. I'm committed to your long‑term success.

Q. Do you only work with certain industries?

I work mainly with service businesses , law firms, contractors, HVAC and roofing companies, travel agencies , but the process works for any business that needs more inquiries from its website.

Q. Do I need technical skills to work with you?

Not at all. I guide you through every step in plain language. You focus on your business; I handle all the technical complexity.

Q. What happens if something breaks after launch?

I'm just a message away. I provide ongoing support to ensure your site stays healthy, secure, and performing at its best.
